How this page is built

Every federal figure on NoOvertimeTax comes from IRS guidance on the qualified overtime deduction and Form 1040 Schedule 1-A, with the FLSA premium definition from 29 U.S.C. 207. The cap, the income phase out, and the 2025 to 2028 window are recomputed in code, never hand typed into the copy. The calculator was checked by hand against the IRS rule and matched to the cent.

Every named state status links to that state's Department of Revenue, its legislation, or an authoritative tracker. A state with no published guidance, or where sources conflict, is shown as not yet confirmed rather than guessed. State positions are changing through 2026, so the page is dated and re-verified.

Not tax advice. Confirm with the IRS, your state Department of Revenue, or a qualified professional before filing.
